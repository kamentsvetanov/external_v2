chi2test

PURPOSE

CHI2TEST Calculate the probability under a chi-square distribution

SYNOPSIS

function p = chi2test(X2,df)

DESCRIPTION 

 CHI2TEST  Calculate the probability under a chi-square distribution
    P = CHI2TEST(X2,DF), where X2 is an observed value and DF is the
    number of degrees of freedom, returns P, the probability of observing
    a value of the chi-square statistic that is at least as extreme as X2.
