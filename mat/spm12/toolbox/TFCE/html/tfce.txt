      <h2>Description</h2>
      <p>This toolbox is a an extension to <a href="matlab:web('http://www.fil.ion.ucl.ac.uk/spm/software/spm12/','-browser');">SPM12</a> (Wellcome Department of Cognitive Neurology) to provide non-parametric statistics based on threshold-free cluster enhancement (TFCE). The idea of the TFCE approach is to combine focal effects with large voxel height as well as broad effects. In contrast to common approaches that consider cluster-based thresholding no initial (and arbitrary) cluster-forming threshold is necessary. Furthermore, TFCE is fairly robust to nonstationarity in the data, which can be often seen in VBM data. </p>
      <p>The toolbox is developed by Christian Gaser (University of Jena, Departments of Psychiatry and Neurology) and free but copyright software, distributed under the terms of the <a href="matlab:web('http://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl-2.0.html','-browser')">GNU General Public Licence</a> as published by the Free Software Foundation; either version 2 of the Licence, or (at your option) any later version. If you use any TFCE code for commercial application, please send a <a href="mailto:christian.gaser@uni-jena.de">mail</a>.</p>
      <p>The toolbox can be applied to (almost) any existing statistical (parametric) SPM design for 3D volume or surface data.</p>
