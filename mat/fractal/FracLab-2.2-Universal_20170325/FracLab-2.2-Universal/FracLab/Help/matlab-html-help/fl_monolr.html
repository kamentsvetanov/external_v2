fl_monolr


The same as monolr, but :

  • a_hat1 and b_hat1 are just scalars (last element of a_hat and b_hat if RegParam=='pls', else first)
  • RegParam can be 'linf' or 'lsup'. In this case, fl_monolr evaluates the liminf or limsup of the slope
  • No other output arguments
